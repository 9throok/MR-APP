Core Features

Everything a field rep needs, in one platform

Built specifically for pharmaceutical sales — from morning planning to end-of-day reporting.

🗺
Intelligent Route Planning
AI-powered daily route optimization that minimizes travel time and maximizes the number of doctors visited per day based on priority scores.
🩺
Doctor Visit Tracking
Log every call with product details discussed, doctor feedback, prescribing trends, and follow-up actions — all with one tap from your mobile.
💊
Sample Management
Real-time inventory of your sample bag, automated acknowledgment from doctors, and compliance-ready digital signatures for every sample issued.
📊
Performance Analytics
Live dashboards showing MTD targets, call averages, coverage rates, and territory-wise performance against benchmarks — visible to reps and managers.
🏥
Territory Management
Visual territory maps with doctor density heatmaps, segmentation by specialty, and coverage gap alerts to ensure no key prescriber is missed.
📋
Smart Call Reporting
Auto-filled daily call reports with GPS check-in, time stamps, and product-wise detailing summaries. Sync with your CRM or ERP at day's end.
